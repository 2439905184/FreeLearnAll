补全对话必背句型

购物

  1. Can I help you? 有什么需要帮助的吗？
  2. What color do you want ? 你想要什么颜色的？
  3. What size do you want ? 你想要什么尺寸的？
  4. How much is it? 多少钱？
  5. Can I try it on? 我能试一下吗？
  6. I'll take ite. 我会买它
  7. What kind of... would you like? 你想要什么种类的...？
  8. Can you show me another? 你能给我换一个/件吗？
  9. Can you make it cheaper? 能便宜点吗？
  10. I want to buy ... for my ... 我想给我的....买...
  11. It fits you well. 非常适合你
  12. Anything else? 还要别的吗？
  13. Is that all? 就这些吗？
  14. That's all, thank you. 就这些，谢谢
  15. Here you are. 给你
  16. Here is your change. 找你的零钱

问路

  1. Excuse me, is there a... near here? 打扰一下，这附近有......吗
  2. Excuse me,can you tell me how to get to ..?打扰一下，你能告诉我怎么到...吗？
  3. Where is a......? 这有一个......吗？
  4. How far is it？有多远？
  5. Sorry,I'm new here too.对不起，我也个新到这的。=I'm a stranger here.
  6. How can I get to... 我怎么能到......?
  7. You'd better take a bus.你最好乘公交车。
  8. Can I go there by...?我能乘坐......到那吗？
  9. Is it far from here？离这远吗？
  10. Which bus can I take？我该乘坐哪辆公交车？
  11. You can't miss it. 你不可能错过它。
  12. It's about 15 minutes' walk. 大约步行15分钟的流程

打电话的常用语

  1. Can I speak to...,please?我能与......通话吗？
  2. He is not here right now. 他现在不在这儿。
  3. Who is that? 你是谁？
  4. Hold on,please 请别挂线。/请稍等。
  5. This is ...(speaking)我是...。
  6. Can I take a message for you? 我能为你捎口信吗？
  7. Can I leave a message? 我能留个口信吗？
  8. I'll tell her as soon as she comes back. 她一回来，我就告诉她。
  9. Could you ask her to call me back? 你要她给我回电吗，好吗？

看病的常用语

  1. What's wrong with you?What's the matter with you? What happened? 你怎么了？
  2. How long have you been like this? 你像这样多久了？
  3. When did it start? 什么时候发病的？
  4. I have a headache/cough/fever. 我得了头痛/咳嗽/发烧。
  5. Is it serious? 严重吗？
  6. It's nothing serious. 没什么严重的。
  7. How often should I take the medicine? 我该多久吃一次药？
  8. Take the medicine three times a day. 一天吃三次这个药。
  9. I hope you'll get better soon. 我希望你会很快好起来。
  10. How are you feeling now? 你现在感觉怎么样？
  11. Much better. 好多了。
  12. I am feeling better now. 我现在感觉好多了
  13. I don't feel very well. 我感觉很不好。
